How to Become a Meesho Seller

Meesho has transformed online reselling in India, empowering millions, especially women, to start businesses from home. Whether you’re a student, homemaker, or entrepreneur, Meesho offers a platform to sell products and earn commissions without any upfront investment.

Why Choose Meesho?

Meesho is a leading e-commerce platform in India with zero joining fees. It provides access to thousands of products at wholesale prices, a user-friendly app, and full support for managing orders and logistics.

Steps to Become a Meesho Seller

1. Download the Meesho app from the Play Store or App Store.

2. Register with your mobile number and provide basic details.

3. Browse product categories and share them on WhatsApp, Facebook, or Instagram.

4. When a customer places an order, add your profit margin and place the order via the app.

5. Meesho handles shipping and delivery, and your profit is deposited into your bank account.

Tips for Success

✔️ Share products regularly on social media to boost visibility.

✔️ Use engaging captions to build trust with your audience.

✔️ Respond promptly and politely to customer inquiries.

✔️ Focus on trending products like fashion, kitchenware, and electronics.
